Discretion note: Smart ATM deposits appear on your banking activity as a cash withdrawal you initiated, with no recipient name displayed.

The remaining balance is paid in cash at the beginning of our time together. For shorter bookings, I may accept an alternative method at my discretion.

How to pay a deposit with Beem It (instant and discreet)

Download or open Beem It, then:

  1. Tap "Pay" and enter the deposit amount.
  2. Use the handle or details I provide in our messages.
  3. Add the note I supply (for example, booking date and time) and confirm.

Transfers are typically instant and show a generic description in banking activity.

How to pay a deposit with Wise

Wise is ideal for fast bank transfers and international clients. Visit wise.com or use the Wise app.

  1. Add a new recipient using the account details I provide.
  2. Enter the exact deposit amount and your booking note (date and time).
  3. Choose your funding method (bank transfer or card) and confirm.

Wise shows minimal reference text and provides a receipt once complete.

How Smart ATM (cardless cash) works

A Smart ATM allows a one-time, code-based cash withdrawal created in your banking app. I will nominate a withdrawal location and a collection window.

  1. In your banking app, generate a cardless cash withdrawal for the deposit amount.
  2. Receive a secure code or reference (and sometimes an SMS PIN).
  3. Share the code and amount with me; I redeem it at the nominated Smart ATM.

Discretion: there are no names on this transfer. Treat the code like cash and share it only with me.

Statement appearance: usually shows as a cash withdrawal you performed at the nominated ATM, with no recipient name.
